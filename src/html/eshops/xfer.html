Xfer

Project description

Client: mime digital s.r.o.
Languages: JavaScript, CSS, HTML
Packages: SASS, Docker, Gulp
Link: www.shop.xfer.cz

Embark on a journey to redefine wholesale fashion retail with this e-commerce venture. We transformed the landscape of bulk clothing orders with innovative features designed to streamline the purchasing process for wholesalers.

Xfer offers a seamless experience for wholesale buyers, allowing the effortless addition of multiple product variants in a single click. This simplifies bulk orders and ensures efficient management of large-scale purchases.

One of the standout features is an advanced cart management system that intelligently splits carts based on expedition dates. Leveraging real-time data from APIs, each product is categorized by its expedition date, optimizing order fulfillment and delivery logistics.

Implementing these cutting-edge functionalities posed unique challenges, requiring extensive customization and bending of the Shoptet platform to meet specific requirements.
