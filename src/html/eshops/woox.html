Woox

Project description

Client: mime digital s.r.o.
Languages: JavaScript, CSS, HTML
Packages: jQuery, SASS, Docker, Gulp
Link: www.woox.cz

Woox revolutionizes online fashion retail in the Czech Republic and Slovakia with its cutting-edge e-commerce platform. Featuring a sleek interface, it offers a diverse range of clothing, from streetwear to formal attire.

A standout feature is its exclusive membership program, providing registered customers with a generous 50% discount on every item along additional discounts. Implementing this required intricate coding to handle variable pricing, ensuring fairness across the platform.

Products boast special properties like insulation and water resistance, catering to diverse needs. Plus, integrated product videos offer an immersive shopping experience, showcasing design and fabric details.

Woox sets the bar high with its commitment to quality, innovation, and personalized customer service, reshaping the way people shop for fashion online.
